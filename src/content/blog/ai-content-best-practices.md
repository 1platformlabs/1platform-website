## Quality Over Quantity

AI content generation has matured significantly, but the key to success remains the same: quality content that serves user intent. Here are our best practices for generating SEO content that actually ranks.

## Start With Keyword Research

Never generate content without a target keyword. Use keyword extraction to identify terms with strong search volume and clear intent before generating a single word.

### Match Search Intent

The most common mistake is generating content that doesn't match what users are actually searching for. A keyword like "best SEO tools" implies a comparison article, not a product page.

## Structure for Both Humans and Search Engines

AI-generated content should follow the same structural best practices as human-written content:

- **One H1 per page** containing your primary keyword
- **Clear H2 sections** that break the article into scannable chunks
- **Short paragraphs** (2-3 sentences) for readability
- **Internal links** to related content on your site

## Review and Edit

AI content is a starting point, not the finish line. Always review generated articles for:

1. Factual accuracy
2. Brand voice consistency
3. Keyword placement and density
4. Internal linking opportunities

## Measure and Iterate

Track your generated content's performance using search analytics. Articles that rank well share common patterns — identify them and feed those patterns back into your content strategy.

## Conclusion

AI content generation works best when combined with solid SEO fundamentals. Use it to scale your content production, but never skip the human review step.
